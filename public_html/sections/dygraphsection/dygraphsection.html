Since 2000, the Colorado River Basin has experienced the driest 16-year period in over 100 years of historical natural flows (Bureau of Reclamation, 2015) and one of the most severe droughts in the last 1,200 years (Meko et al., 2007a and 2007b).

Bureau of Reclamation, 2015. Colorado River Basin Natural Flow and Salt Data website.

Meko, D.M., et al. 2007a. Upper Colorado River Flow Reconstruction. IGBP PAGES/World Data Center for Paleoclimatology. Data Contribution Series # 2007-052. NOAA/NCDC Paleoclimatology Program, Boulder CO, USA.

Meko, D.M., C.A. Woodhouse, C.H. Baisan, T. Knight, J.J. Lukas, M.K. Hughes, and M.W. Salzer. 2007b. Medieval drought in the upper Colorado River Basin. Geophysical Research Letters, Vol. 34, L10705.
