The Colorado River is a Lifeline

Water from the Colorado River is essential for the life in the southwest United States and Mexico. Key River Facts:

  • Supplies more than 1 in 10 Americans water for municipal use, including for drinking water, including 22 federally recognized tribes
  • Supports 4,200 megawatts of electrical generating capacity
  • Provides irrigation for more than 5.5 million acres (~8,590 square miles) of land
  • Serves 7 National Wildlife Refuges, 4 National Recreation Areas, and 11 National Parks

Data source: Bureau of Reclamation Colorado River Basin Study, 2013
